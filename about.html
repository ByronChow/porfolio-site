BIO

Byron Chow recently completed his Artist Residency at NYU Steinhardt, and is currently based in New York City working as a freelance designer.

Previous work experience includes Art Direction and graphics for J Crew, Viacom, UnderArmour, Opening Ceremony and The Doneger Group.
His other personal interests include street art, animal facts, hats (of all shapes and sizes), trying strange foods, and the pursuit of happiness.

For inquiries, resume request or additional information, feel free to contact him at byronchow7@gmail.com. He is always looking to collaborate and work with like-minded individuals.

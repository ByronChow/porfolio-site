Based in Lowell Massachussetts (known as the Cradle of the Industrial Revolution), The Museum of American Textiles is located in a converted factory space and is home to the largest collection of textile prints and Industrial-era textile machines in the United States. The focal point of the rebrand is to highlight the archive, inviting visitors to easily browse the comprehensive catalogue.
